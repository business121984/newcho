Your Neighbourhood Asian Grocery

NEW CHỢ (New Market) brings the freshness and variety of a traditional Asian market to Melbourne. We stock products from Vietnam, Korea, Japan, the Philippines, China and beyond.

From fresh daily produce to hard-to-find pantry staples, frozen meals and specialty drinks — we make it easy to cook authentic Asian food at home.
